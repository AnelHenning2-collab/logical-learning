Exam Tips — SY0-701
Know the "Why"Questions often test why you choose one control over another, not just definitions. Understand tradeoffs.
Scenario-BasedMost questions present a real-world scenario. Map symptoms → attack type → mitigation before reading answers.
NIST FrameworkKnow Identify → Protect → Detect → Respond → Recover. Many questions map to this lifecycle.
Acronym TrapIDS vs IPS, DAC vs MAC vs RBAC, symmetric vs asymmetric — examiners love look-alike pairs. Drill these.
