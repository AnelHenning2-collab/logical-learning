For learners · how to use this

Follow the map Pick your current domain in Security+, then move through topics in order instead of jumping randomly.
Take your own notes Use the Cornell layout that inspired these labs or your own system, but always write in your own words.
AI with integrity It’s fine to ask AI to explain ideas. It’s not okay to paste AI answers into graded work—always follow your school’s policy.
Tell me what’s missing If a topic isn’t clear or a lab idea would help you, reach out via the Contact Hub and I’ll add it to the roadmap.
